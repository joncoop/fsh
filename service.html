FSH Maintenance, LLC is the largest, authorized Robinson Service Center in the Southeast United States. We are also an FAA certified 145 Repair Center (FAA 145 Station #6FSR721B). Our highly-trained, certified mechanics are here to help you with all your maintenance needs. FSH is the the leading provider of R66 maintenance in the United States.

We recently relocated (March, 2016) to a bigger, 13,000 square foot hangar to better serve our customer's needs and to continue our company’s steady growth. Included in our new facility is a large climate-controlled maintenance and inventory storage room to house our tools as well as our extensive parts department. Our inventory is comprised of parts for the R66, R44, and R22 airframes, as well as parts for the Rolls Royce RR300 Engine and Lycoming Engines. We can provide for overnight shipping on many items.
