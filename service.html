FSH Maintenance, LLC has been offering premier helicopter maintenance and service for over 13 years. We are an FAA certified 145 Repair Center (FAA 145 Station #6FSR721B) and authorized Robinson Service Center. Our trained and certified mechanics are here to help you with all your maintenance needs.

We have recently relocated (March, 2016) to a bigger facility to better satisfy our customer's needs and our company’s steady growth. Included with our new facility is a large climate controlled inventory storage room to house our extensive parts and tools. Our inventory comprises parts for the R66, R44, and R22 airframes, as well as parts for the Rolls Royce 300 Engine and Lycoming Engines.

Your safety is our #1 goal. With our comprehensive thorough inspections and maintenance we are able to accomplish this goal leaving our customers satisfied with a safe, clean, and well maintained aircraft for every job. For more information and pricing please feel free to contact us.

Services available include:

  • R22, R44, R66 Annuals/100-hr inspections
  • R66 200 hr and 400 hr inspections
  • Overhauls
  • Track and Balance
  • RR300 Compressor Wash/Rinse
  • Oil/Filter Changes
  • And much more…
