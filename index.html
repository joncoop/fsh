Sales & Service

Florida Suncoast Helicopters, an authorized Robinson Helicopter dealership, will provide you with knowledgeable service as you look to purchase a new or pre-owned helicopter. In addition to selling the Robinson R22 and R44, the company is Florida’s only authorized dealer of the new R66. Please contact us to talk about your new helicopter today!

We are also an FAA certified 145 Repair Center and authorized Robinson Service Center. Our highly-trained, certified mechanics are here to help you with all your maintenance needs. Our comprehensive inspections and maintenance leave our customers satisfied with a safe, clean, and well-maintained aircraft after every job. Please contact us for more information and pricing.
