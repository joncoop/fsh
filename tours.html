Take flight along Sarasota’s coast to see its unique downtown architecture, spectacular beachfront homes, world-famous white sand beaches, and marine wildlife.

Almost immediately upon departure, two notable, local landmarks come into view: The John and Mable Ringling Art Museum and, Ca D’Zan, their recently restored Venetian style mansion on the bay. Continuing along the Bayfront, another unusual building stands out. The very purple building is the Van Wezel Performing Arts Hall.

A turn to the West takes the flight over the Ringling Bridge and out to St. Armands Circle and Lido Key.

Passengers on the thirty-minute tour can choose either the southern or northern routes. Our southern tour travels along the coast of Siesta Key and Casey Key to the Venice Inlet and makes it return to the airport by flying along the bayfront at Big Pass and over Bird Key, while the northern tour flies along the coast of Longboat Key.

Those choosing a one-hour tour will have the opportunity to see virtually the entire Sarasota coastline, including sites from both the northern and southern tours.

FAQs

Can I bring a camera?

Yes, please do. You may bring a still or video cameras.

What if I have to cancel?

We require a 24 hour notice for cancellations. If you cancel your flight after then we will charge you a $50 fee for a half hour tour and a $100 fee for an hour tour.

What if the weather is bad?

If the weather conditions will not allow us to fly then we will be happy to reschedule your tour without any fee.

Can my kids come for a tour?

Yes, but they must be able to sit without a child restraint and in their own seat.

What about the Grandparents?

Of course they can. The helicopter can be difficult to get in and out of with decreased mobility, but it does not mean you have to stay on the ground. Believe it or not we have had a passenger take a ride for his 100th birthday.

What if the weather is bad?

If the weather conditions will not allow us to fly then we will be happy to reschedule your tour without any fee.

How often do the tours depart?

We have not set departure schedule. All you need to do is call and set up a time and date that suits you best. Tours after normal business hours may be subject to an extra charge.

Am I supposed to tip the pilot?

The pilot usually receives a tip for the tour.

Can I give a ride as a gift?

People have purchased rides for tours, first lessons, engagement proposals, and more. Personalized gift certificates are available.
